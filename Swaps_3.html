Reusable Water Bottles

Reusable metal or glass bottles instead of plastic water bottles.

Why Make This Swap?

  • Reduces single-use plastic waste.
  • Can be more durable than plastic bottles.
  • Some bottles offer temperature control features.
  • Contributes to a healthier environment.

How to Use

Simply fill the bottle with water, secure the lid, and drink.

Where to Find

You can find reusable water bottles at:

  • Local eco-friendly stores
  • Eco-conscious retailers
  • Online boutiques

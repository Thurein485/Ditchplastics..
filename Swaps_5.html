Shampoo Bars

Shampoo bars are solid blocks of shampoo that work just like liquid shampoo but without the plastic bottle.

Why Make This Swap?

  • Reduces single-use plastic waste
  • Shampoo bars last longer and are easier to travel with
  • Come in simple, eco-friendly packaging
  • Help support a cleaner, healthier planet

How to Use

Wet your hair and the shampoo bar, then rub it between your hands or directly on your scalp to create lather. Rinse as usual.

Where to Find

  • Local eco-friendly stores
  • Sustainable product retailers
  • Online green shops

Glass Bottle Vase

Transform an empty glass bottle into a pretty flower vase for your home.

Steps:

  1. Clean and dry an empty glass bottle.
  2. Remove any labels using warm water and soap.
  3. Decorate the bottle with paint, twine, or stickers.
  4. Fill with fresh water and add flowers.
  5. Display it on your table or window sill.
